Later that day...
Their father wasted no time to plan the family's future.
His instinct was to move immediately to a new location.
This of course meaned abandoning Gregor to rot away in his room.
Since the mother was too fragile for anything, it was up to Grete to debate on the situation.
Eventually, Grete convinced her parents that she was to take care of her brother until his recovery.
That morning she brought him several of his favorite foods.
Certainly she would not have wanted Gregor to starve to death!
Within their first encounter, Grete heared what she thought were the cries of her sickly brother.
Hearing his voice, together with her love for her brother;
Grete decided to remain close to Gregor.
