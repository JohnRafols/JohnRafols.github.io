Months later have passed.
Ideally, the parents would have gotten jobs. But, the father realized a much easier option.
Why work when you can have someone else to do it for you?
Of course it wasn't an ideal situation.
To top it all off, the lodger wasn't a lovely man.
As for Gregor...
Often he crept up against the window to look out. From day to day he perceived things with less and less clarity.
He found it difficult to bear lying quitely during the night. So for diversion he acquired the habit of crawling back and forth across the walls and ceiling. The experience was quite different from lying on the floor. It was easier from him to breathe.
Each morning his sister would note this amusement of his.
For as he crept around he left behind here and there traces of his sticky stuff.
One night, she got the idea of making Gregor's creeping around as easy as possible
She thought of removing the furniture in his room which got in the way.
As Grete removed furniture one by one,
she did not realize that Gregor was watching the whole time.
Of course a 17 year old girl was in no position to move heavy furniture by herself.
...How unfortunate of a position to be in.
That night, Grete waited until she heard no signs of Gregor's anthics.
Gregor believed that by removing the furniture; she has shown him that she was giving up all hope of his improvement; and is leaving him to his own resources without any consideration.

"Nothing was to be removed; everything must remain."
