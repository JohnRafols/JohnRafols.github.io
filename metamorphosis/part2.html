Once the weather cleared that day Gregor's father laid out all the financial circumstances and prospects to his mother and to his sister.

Knowing full well that Gregor is no longer in position to work for the family, Gregor's father planned to move immediately to a new location.
Eventually, Grete convinced her parents that she was to take care of Gregor, until his hopefull recovery.
Gregor never could have guessed what his sister would do goodness of her heart.
Later that afternoon, she brought him, to test his taste, an entire selection of food.
Certainly she would not have her own brother starve to death!
As she entered his room, Grete did not find him immediately,
she made the assumption that he was under the bed.
Within their first encounter, Grete heared what she thought were the cries of her sickly brother.
With the belief that this it truly him;
Grete decided to remain close to Gregor.
